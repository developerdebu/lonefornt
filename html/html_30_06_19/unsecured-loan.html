Looking for unsecured loan?

An instant loan from LoanFront is the answer.

An unsecured loan is a loan that is granted without you needing to show an asset or a collateral to back it up. This makes the loan application process easy and quick – in fact, with our LoanFront app, you can even complete the whole process online. Unlike secured loans, these loans do not require extensive paperwork or documentation. In fact, we only require you to upload a selfie, a photo of your PAN card and any one government ID.

Unsecured loans can be a great option when you need some cash in a hurry, and would prefer to avoid a tedious, long-drawn process. It is also ideal for comparatively smaller loan amounts that do not call for an asset pledge of any kind. At LoanFront, you can get a cash advance of ₹2,000 to ₹10,000, completely unsecured.
