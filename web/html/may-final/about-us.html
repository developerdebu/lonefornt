About LoanFront

LoanFront is a single point app that helps you get quick, easy loans, starting from amounts as small as ₹2000 and going right up to ₹2 Lakhs. Our app is digitally driven from end to end. This means from filling out your details and submitting your documents to verification and disbursal – the entire loan application process is carried out online.

Apart from convenience, our digital processes mean that we can offer unbelievable speed of delivery to your bank account. There’s yet another way we streamline the process to make easy cash loans available to you. Once you fill out your information and upload your documents the first time, you do not have to go through that process ever again. When you need your next loan, simply log in and apply!

Trust and reliability are at the heart of everything we do, so we offer transparency at each step. From telling you why we need access to each feature on your device to letting you know the exact loan amount you will receive, we ensure that there are no surprises at any point.

Our Vision

Applying for a loan is usually seen as a complicated, tedious process. Think long queues, lots of paperwork, and a nail-biting wait. This is not a desirable situation when you need money urgently for an emergency. This is also not something that most people would bother to go through, when they just need small cash loans until their salary arrives.

That’s where we come in. Our goal is to do away with all the complexities of applying for loans, and make the process as smooth, seamless and hassle-free as possible. That is why, our online loan application process is as simple as swipe, click and upload.

We aim to be your first port of call, anytime you require loans – no matter how small or large. We aim to be at the forefront of your financial needs, always.
